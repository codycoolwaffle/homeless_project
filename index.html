What is this?

This website intends to serve as a useful tool for homeless people themselves. It provides advice for the homeless like tips on how to survive in a cold night and a link to a directory for homeless shelters. We also provide tips for ways to stay healthy.

What is the Homeless Directory?

The Homeless Shelter Directory provides Homeless Shelters and Homeless Service Organizations. The directory was created for people who want to find and donate food and/or supplies to their local shelter.
